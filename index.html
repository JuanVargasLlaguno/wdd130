Uruguay

Uruguay is a country of only 3M people, situated on South America between two of the largest countries in the world: Brazil and Argentina. Nonetheless, Uruguay has managed to achieve incredible feats at world level. Most notably Uruguay's national soccer team has won the World Cup 2 times (with other 2 World titles prior) and is also the current champion on the sub-20 category, despite having less population on the whole country than some nearby cities like Buenos Aires. The capital, Montevideo, is at the south of the country, bordering with the Rio de la Plata. It has amazing beaches and many turistic spots. 53% of Uruguay's population lives in Montevideo.
